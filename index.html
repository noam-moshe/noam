America is almost the same land size as Australia,
but, unlike Australia, the interior is also filled in with many things to do.
Careful research and planning will help you create your dream USA travel experience.
Consider what it is you really want to see and do.

Are you focused on:


  • National Parks (like Yosemite National Park, or the Grand Canyon)
  • Theme parks (like Disneyland or Universal Studios Orlando)
  • Major cities
  • Road trips (like Southern California)
  • Beaches or mountains
  • Food or drink
  • Festivals and Events
  • Diverse cultures
  • History or music scene (like in Nashville or Memphis)
